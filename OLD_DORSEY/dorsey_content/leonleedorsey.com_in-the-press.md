## Cantaloupe Island

![A man with braided hair standing and leaning against a wall in an unfinished room with a cello hanging on the wall behind him.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/61e8380ff106b645a8ea95bd/97c7e03e-3afc-431a-bfd9-9b3acc2972b5/cantaloupe-island.png)

“Renowned bassist Leon Lee Dorsey continues his prolific streak with the issue of _Cantaloupe Island_, the latest album to be released on his Jazz Avenue 1 label and a continuation of his bountiful partnership with revered drummer Mike Clark.

On their sixth collaboration since 2019, the stalwart rhythm section brings in virtuosic guitarist Russell Malone for an invigorating nine-track program of compositions by Horace Silver, Wes Montgomery, Prince and more.”

– [Lydia Liebman](https://lydialiebman.com/index.php/project/leon-lee-dorsey/)

## Blues on Top

![Book cover titled 'Blues on Top' featuring a blurred image of a person's face against a blue background, with authors Mike Clark, Leon Lee Dorsey, and Mike Ledonne.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/61e8380ff106b645a8ea95bd/1718811171578-UR65MTUGFCGYOXDLOHII/Blues-on-top.png)

“The trio … tears through a brief but blazing rendition of Duke Ellington’s _C Jam Blues_, paced by Dorsey’s impeccable walking, which is right out of the Leroy Vinnegar/Israel Crosby school.

LeDonne responds by channeling his inner Bud Powell-Art Tatum on this extraordinary chops showcase.”

– [Lydia Liebman](https://lydialiebman.com/index.php/project/leon-lee-dorsey#pg-31469-1)

## Freedom Jazz Dance

![Album cover titled 'Freedom Jazz Dance' with the artist names Leonard Dorsey, Manuel Valera, and Mike Clarke in white at the bottom, against a blue brick wall background.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/61e8380ff106b645a8ea95bd/1643036874036-FBZZT7VVAG2RP9QHV9FE/Leon-Lee-Dorsey-Freedom-Jazz-Dance-CD-cover-copy-scaled.jpeg)

**JOHN CHACONA**"Pittsburgh-born bassist Leon Lee Dorsey has had a busy 2021."

“While the individual virtuosity of Dorsey, Clark and Valera is undeniable throughout _Freedom Jazz Dance_, it’s their irrepressible chemistry that wins over listeners. It’s a classic example where the sum of the parts is greater than the whole.”

– [Lydia Liebman](https://lydialiebman.com/index.php/project/leon-lee-dorsey/)

## Thank You Mr. Maebern!

![Mr. Dorsey in a black suit holding a double bass and looking at the camera, with blue text thanking Mr. Mabern and listing the names Leon Lee Dorsey, Harold Mabern, and Mike Clark.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/61e8380ff106b645a8ea95bd/1644176192578-G2GBEE48TOMG4YTCF21H/thank-you-mr-mabern-1-1-e1607630296620.jpg)

JIM HYNES **MAKING A SCENE**

"Bassist Leon Lee Dorsey and legendary drummer Mike Clark are a tandem that just keeps on chugging, raising curiosity as to who will be the third member to join them on their next outing."

“ ‘Thank You, Mr. Mabern!’ documents the ingenuity, openness, and soulfulness trademark to Harold Mabern’s sound. The album captures a celebration of Mabern’s influences and legacy and stands as an inimitable union of these three consummate players. The rare magic evoked is chronicled in all of its vibrancy on this album’s nine tracks.”

– [Lydia Liebman](https://lydialiebman.com/index.php/project/leon-lee-dorsey/)

## Sgt. Pepper

![Album cover featuring three men dressed as 1970s military officers with exaggerated costumes, with the text 'WOLFF CLARK DORSEY' at the top and 'PLAY SGT. PEPPER' at the bottom.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/61e8380ff106b645a8ea95bd/1642707567361-34Y4W2PG9CE8KYXM6884/releases-sgt-pepper-cover.jpg)

D. OSCAR GROOMES **O'S PLACE**

"Bassist Leon Lee Dorsey is a solid bandleader.."

“Wolff's bluesy, singing, and always swinging lines have echoes here of Ahmad Jamal, Horace Silver and the soulful Bobby Timmons, and the fetching rhythm with Clark and Dorsey set the music on a joyful journey.”

\- All About Jazz: Lisa Reedy Promotions

“It was absolutely a magical experience recording with two such extraordinary musicians like Michael Wolff and Mike Clark on such an iconic piece of music history from a legendary band like the Beatles.”

\- Leon Lee Dorsey

## Monktime

![Colorful abstract painting of a man wearing sunglasses, with text 'DSC - MONKTIME' at the top and band members' names at the bottom.](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/61e8380ff106b645a8ea95bd/1642966893130-DIOHSHXSJ97BD76XMXOF/releases-monktime-cover.jpg)

D. OSCAR GROOMES **O'S PLACE**

"Bassist Leon Lee Dorsey is a solid bandleader.."

“ _MonkTime_ is Leon Lee Dorsey's first album as a leader in twenty years and in this case, patience truly is a virtue: The bassist's tribute to the legendary composer and pianist simultaneously debuts Dorsey's new band, a trio with guitarist Greg Skaff and drummer Mike Clark, the standard for contemporary jazz-rock and jazz-funk drumming who sticks to pure jazz drumming in unique _MonkTime_.”

“It's no great revelation that even Monk's most "standard" tunes, such as "Blue Monk" or "Well You Needn't," can sound difficult or at least odd, and that playing such music on completely different instruments can at least potentially present additional challenges. But this trio approaches each tune with great skill and honorable intent, and their music is powerful.“

– All About Jazz: Chris M. Slawecki
