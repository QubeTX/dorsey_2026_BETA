## How It’s Made

Immerse yourself in the artistic fusion of jazz mastery and artisanal craftsmanship.

Celebrated bassist, **Leon Lee Dorsey**, meets his match in **Seth Kimmel**, a luthier of extraordinary talent from Eugene, Oregon.

Witness the birth of Dorsey's newest _Redwood Upright Bass_ \- a symphony of Kimmel's unique tonal exploration and refined skill​. This is luxury redefined, a dance of tradition and innovation: it's how it's made.
