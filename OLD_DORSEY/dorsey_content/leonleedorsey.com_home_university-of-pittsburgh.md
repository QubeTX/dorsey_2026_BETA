_Leon Lee Dorsey, acclaimed jazz bassist and Berklee School of Music educator, has performed with jazz legends and released multiple albums, including the recent "A Letter to Bill Evans." Founder of Leon Lee Dorsey Studios in NYC, his influence on jazz is profound and ever-enduring._
