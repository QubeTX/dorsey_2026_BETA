# Blues On Top

Jun 17

Written By [Emmett Shaughnessy](https://leonleedorsey.com/new-releases?author=61e837ad6358fb7f47db46c2)

Blending the soulful depths of blues with the improvisational spirit of jazz, acclaimed bassist and composer **Leon Lee Dorsey** unveils his latest album, **“Blues On Top.”** This electrifying collection features the legendary drummer **Mike Clark** and the renowned **Mike LeDonne** on the Hammond B3 organ, marking a significant addition to contemporary jazz discography.

> _“Our goal with ‘Blues On Top’ was to honor the rich heritage of blues while infusing it with fresh energy and our unique perspectives,”_ shares **Leon Lee Dorsey**. _“Collaborating with Mike Clark and Mike LeDonne brought out the best in all of us, resulting in a sound that’s both timeless and forward-thinking.”_

**A Dynamic Trio Continues Their Musical Journey**

“Blues On Top” is the fifth consecutive trio project by Dorsey and Clark, following acclaimed collaborations with artists like **Manuel Valera**, **Harold Mabern**, **Michael Wolff**, and **Greg Skaff**. This album stands out as **Mike LeDonne** brings his exceptional Hammond B3 organ skills to the forefront, adding a rich, textured layer to the trio’s sound.

**Album Highlights:**

- **“Blues On Top”**: The title track, penned by **Mike LeDonne**, sets an energetic tone with its soulful grooves and compelling melodies.

- **“Stolen Moments”**: A fresh take on **Oliver Nelson’s** classic, showcasing the trio’s seamless interplay and innovative arrangements.

- **“Willow Weep for Me”**: An unexpected, up-tempo rendition of **Ann Ronell’s** standard, highlighting the group’s ability to reinterpret and revitalize timeless pieces.

- **“C Jam Blues”**: A spirited homage to **Duke Ellington**, filled with dynamic solos and rhythmic sophistication.

- **“Jacob’s Ladder”**: A nod to **Cedar Walton**, featuring intricate harmonies and engaging improvisations.

- **“Can’t Buy Me Love”**: A blues-infused transformation of **The Beatles’** hit, demonstrating the trio’s versatility and creative prowess.


**Mike LeDonne’s** exclusive focus on the Hammond B3 organ in this project adds a distinctive voice to the album. His vibrant playing complements **Dorsey’s** melodic bass lines and **Clark’s** dynamic drumming, creating a cohesive and captivating soundscape.

> _“Recording ‘Blues On Top’ was an exhilarating experience,”_ says **Mike LeDonne**. _“Leon and Mike Clark are phenomenal musicians who bring immense passion and creativity to the studio. Together, we’ve crafted something truly special.”_

**A Celebration of Rhythm and Melody**

Known for their rhythmic expertise, **Dorsey** and **Clark** provide a solid foundation that allows each track to flourish. Their synergy with **LeDonne** enables the trio to explore new musical territories while staying true to the essence of blues and jazz.

> _“The chemistry among us was instantaneous,”_ remarks **Mike Clark**. _“We pushed each other to new heights, and I believe that energy is palpable throughout the album.”_

**About Leon Lee Dorsey:**

With a career spanning over three decades, **Leon Lee Dorsey** is a distinguished bassist and composer who has left an indelible mark on the jazz world. A graduate of the Berklee College of Music and the Manhattan School of Music, Dorsey has collaborated with legends such as **Lionel Hampton**, **Freddie Hubbard**, and **Art Blakey**. His work is celebrated for its innovative approach and deep reverence for jazz traditions.

**Experience “Blues On Top”:**

“Blues On Top” invites listeners on a journey through the rich landscapes of blues and jazz, reimagined by three of the genre’s most talented musicians. The album is now available on all major streaming platforms and at select music retailers.
