# Blue's On Top






$9.99





Delve into the rich tapestry of jazz with "Blues On Top," the latest stellar release from Leon Lee Dorsey, Mike Clark, and Mike LeDonne. This album, which debuted on August 12, 2022, under Jazz Avenue 1, marks the fifth collaboration of Dorsey and Clark, who have continually captivated the jazz scene with their dynamic synergy and inventive musical explorations.

"Blues On Top" opens with a vibrant title track and takes you through a sophisticated journey with classics like Oliver Nelson’s "Stolen Moments" and Dizzy Gillespie's "Birk's Works," each reinterpreted with fresh vibrancy and deep musical intellect. Noteworthy is their transformative cover of The Beatles' "Can’t Buy Me Love," infused with bluesy overtones and electrifying solos that showcase the trio’s exceptional ability to swing and groove.

Each track on the album is a testament to the trio's chemistry and virtuosity, with Mike LeDonne’s dynamic presence on the keys enriching the foundational rhythms laid by Dorsey and Clark. From the swift beats to melodic bass lines, this album is a confluence of individual mastery and collective brilliance, reflecting the profound heritage of jazz music.

"Blues On Top" is not just an album; it's a celebration of jazz's evolving nature, a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary flair, promising to resonate with jazz aficionados and new listeners alike. Explore the depths of jazz through the hands of these maestros who continue to redefine the boundaries of this timeless genre.
