# Cantaloupe Island

Jun 17

Written By [Emmett Shaughnessy](https://leonleedorsey.com/new-releases?author=61e837ad6358fb7f47db46c2)

Acclaimed bassist and composer **Leon Lee Dorsey** proudly announces the release of his latest album, **“Cantaloupe Island,”** under his label **Jazz Avenue 1**. This exceptional project features legendary drummer **Mike Clark** and virtuoso guitarist **Russell Malone**, culminating in a masterful trio that pushes the boundaries of contemporary jazz.

Drawing inspiration from jazz luminaries such as **Horace Silver**, **Wes Montgomery**, and **Herbie Hancock**, “Cantaloupe Island” is a nine-track journey that seamlessly blends elements of jazz, soul, and funk. The album showcases the trio’s remarkable synergy, with each artist contributing their unique voice to create a rich tapestry of immersive sounds.

> _“Collaborating with Mike and Russell was an inspiring experience,”_ says **Leon Lee Dorsey**. _“We aimed to honor the traditions of jazz while infusing it with our own contemporary interpretations. The result is a collection of tracks that we’re incredibly proud of.”_

From the vibrant reinterpretation of **Herbie Hancock’s** iconic **“Cantaloupe Island”** to the soulful grooves of **Horace Silver’s** **“Sister Sadie,”** the album captivates listeners with its seamless interplay and inventive improvisations. **Mike Clark’s** impeccable drumming lays a dynamic foundation, **Dorsey’s** melodic bass lines provide depth and resonance, and **Malone’s** harmonically sophisticated guitar artistry bridges the gap between tradition and innovation.

_“The chemistry we had in the studio was something special,”_ adds **Russell Malone**. _“Leon and Mike bring such energy and creativity to the table, and I believe listeners will feel that vibrancy throughout the album.”_

**Album Highlights:**

- **“Cantaloupe Island”**: A fresh take on Hancock’s classic, infused with modern grooves and intricate solos.

- **“Sister Sadie”**: A soulful homage to Horace Silver, showcasing the trio’s tight-knit synergy.

- **Original Compositions**: New works that highlight the trio’s compositional prowess and collective artistry.


**Experience “Cantaloupe Island”:**

Immerse yourself in the enchanting sounds of “Cantaloupe Island” and witness the magic of this extraordinary trio. The album is available now on all major streaming platforms and in select music stores nationwide.
