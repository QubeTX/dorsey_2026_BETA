# [A Letter to Bill Evans](https://leonleedorsey.com/new-releases/a-letter-to-bill-evans)

Renowned bassist **Leon Lee Dorsey** announces the release of _Letter to Bill Evans_, a heartfelt tribute album featuring drummer Mike Clark and pianist Michael Wolff. Together, they reimagine Bill Evans' timeless classics with fresh interpretations— from a bossa-flavored "Gloria's Step" to swinging versions of "My Romance" and "Nardis." Wolff's personal connection to Evans adds profound depth to this project, making it a must-listen for jazz enthusiasts. Experience this captivating homage when it releases on March 8, 2024, under the _Jazz Avenue 1_ label.
